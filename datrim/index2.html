About Our Academy

Established in 2009, Gopalan Cricket Academy has been at the forefront of cricket training in Bangalore. We are committed to developing technically sound cricketers with strong character and sportsmanship.

Our training methodology blends traditional coaching techniques with modern sports science. We focus on building solid fundamentals while also developing advanced skills for competitive play.

Our facility features professional-grade cricket pitches, bowling machines, video analysis technology, and a dedicated team of former first-class cricketers as coaches.
